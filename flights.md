## Tokyo Airport: Prefer Haneda (HND) over Narita (NRT)
- HND → central Tokyo: ~35 min by train
- NRT → central Tokyo: ~60–90 min by train
- Same price range, HND is much more convenient

---

## Tips
- **Book ASAP** — 3–4 weeks out, fares spike in final 2–3 weeks for international
- **April 20–30 is ideal timing** — shoulder season right before Golden Week (starts May 3)
- **JAL / ANA economy** noticeably better than US carriers for 13+ hr Pacific legs
- **Air Canada via Vancouver** = shortest connection time to Tokyo (~10–11 hrs from YVR)
