## Top 5 Things to Know

1. **Visit Japan Web tonight** — vjw-lp.digital.go.jp. Register before you fly for fast immigration. Takes 10 min.
2. **Suica on your phone** — Add to Apple/Google Pay tonight. Tap in/out of every train and spend at every convenience store.
3. **Cash is king** — Many restaurants and shops are cash-only. Withdraw ¥30,000–50,000 from a 7-Eleven ATM when you land.
4. **Do not tip.** Ever. It can be considered rude in Japan.
5. **You will walk 15,000–25,000 steps/day** — comfortable shoes are mandatory.
